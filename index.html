About Me

Namaste, I'm Akkhil Kapoor.

I am a passionate educator, trainer, and management professional with more than 20 years of experience in the fields of education, information technology, business management, and corporate training. Throughout my career, I have dedicated myself to helping students, professionals, and organizations achieve their goals through quality education, practical knowledge, and continuous learning.

I strongly believe that education is one of the most powerful tools for personal and professional growth. Over the years, I have trained thousands of students from different educational backgrounds and age groups. My teaching philosophy focuses on simplifying complex concepts, encouraging practical learning, and building confidence among learners so that they can successfully apply their knowledge in real-world situations.

My journey in the education sector began more than two decades ago. Since then, I have worked with several reputed institutes in Ambala and have gained extensive experience in teaching computer applications, accounting software, information technology, web development, graphic designing, and multimedia-related subjects. During this period, I have had the privilege of mentoring students who later established successful careers in various industries.
